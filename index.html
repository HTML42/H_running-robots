Welcome!

The basic idea is, the player controls a robots, which runs from left to right like in the old mario bros. games. Main-target-group are mobile gamers. By clicking of the top-, left-, right-, bottom- part of the device, the player can make his robot run faster, slower, make it jump or doing a barrel roll. While running with his robot throught the level he must dodge elements, objects and traps.
